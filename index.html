Admissions

Admission Process for 2025-26

We welcome aspiring students to join our college. The admission process is designed to be straightforward and accessible.

  1. Step 1: Application Submission: Fill out the online application form with all required details.
  2. Step 2: Document Verification: Submit necessary documents (mark sheets, ID proof, etc.) as specified.
  3. Step 3: Entrance Exam/Interview: Qualified candidates may be invited for an entrance exam or interview.
  4. Step 4: Admission Confirmation: Successful candidates will receive an admission offer. Complete fee payment to confirm.

Eligibility Criteria

Admission to Shri Fattechand Jain Jr College for the 11th and 12th standards is based on the following criteria:

  • **Academic Performance:** Minimum 60% marks in the 10th standard (or equivalent examination) from a recognized board. Specific streams may require higher percentages in relevant subjects.
  • **Age Limit:** Applicants must be between 15 and 17 years of age as of June 1st of the admission year. (Relaxation for certain categories as per government norms).
  • **Entrance Exam:** All candidates must appear for the college's entrance examination, which assesses aptitude in core subjects relevant to their chosen stream.
  • **Interview:** Shortlisted candidates from the entrance exam will be called for a personal interview to assess their communication skills, general knowledge, and motivation.
  • **Document Verification:** Original mark sheets, transfer certificates, and identity proofs are required for verification during the admission process.

For detailed eligibility requirements and specific subject prerequisites, please refer to the official college prospectus.
